About Us

Founder's Story: James Kajawo

My name is James Kajawo, and my journey is one of hope and determination. Growing up in a rural village, I knew firsthand the struggles of poverty and limited opportunities. Despite the challenges, I was fortunate to have a sponsor, Mrs. Mary Helen Garvin and her friends in Canada, who believed in me and supported my education.

Inspired by their kindness, I founded the One For All Organization Technical School — a charity providing free practical training to youth who dropped out of school due to poverty.

Our vision is to empower young people with skills to break the cycle of poverty. We believe everyone deserves a chance to succeed, regardless of background.

At One For All, we're not just teaching skills — we're transforming lives. Join us in building a brighter, self-sustaining future for all.

Our Mission

To empower youth through skills training, education, and support, enabling them to achieve economic independence and contribute positively to their communities.

Our Vision

By 2030, we aim to empower more young people to achieve their dreams, reducing the impact of poverty and creating sustainable community transformation.

Our Programs

Skills Training

Our vocational training programs equip youth with practical skills for employment and entrepreneurship.

Electrical Installation
Tailoring & Fashion Design
Welding & Metal Fabrication
Carpentry & Woodworking
Plumbing
Machining

Training includes both theoretical knowledge and hands-on practice with modern equipment.

Education Support

We provide essential educational materials and mentorship to ensure students can focus on learning.

  • Distribution of notebooks, pens, and textbooks to needy students
  • Mentorship programs connecting students with professionals
  • Counseling services for academic and personal challenges
  • After-school tutoring and homework assistance

Our education support program has reached over 15,000 students across 52 villages.

Music Training

Our music program nurtures artistic talent and provides creative outlets for youth expression.

Instrument training (guitar, keyboard, drums)
Music theory and composition
Recording studio access
Performance opportunities

The program includes a fully equipped recording studio where students can produce their music.

Community Support

We engage with the broader community to create an ecosystem that supports youth development.

  • Parent sensitization on the importance of education
  • Financial literacy workshops for families
  • Rural financial access initiatives
  • Community events promoting youth achievements

By involving parents and community leaders, we ensure sustainable support for our students.
